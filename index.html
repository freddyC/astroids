          Help

          Click on the U.S. and Roman coins to earn points. Avoid the Canadian

          coins, as clicking on them will reset your score to zero. Clicking on

          the clock will increase the number coins that will drop during that level.

          You must earn at least 100 points to proceed to the next level.
